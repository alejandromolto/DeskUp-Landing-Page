What's Deskup?

DeskUp is a lightweight, desktop application that lets you save and load your workspaces instantly. Whether you work with multiple terminals, editors, or applications, DeskUp helps you quickly restore your setup and keep your workflow organized.

Techologies used

DeskUp was built thanks to the help of powerful modern technologies.

Open Source

DeskUp is open source, giving you full transparency and control over how your workspaces are saved, loaded, and managed. If you'd like to change something, we are open to contributions!
