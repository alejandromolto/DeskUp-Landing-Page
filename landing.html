What's Deskup?

DeskUp is a lightweight, desktop application that lets you save and load your workspaces instantly. Whether you work with multiple terminals, editors, or applications.

Innovative features

With DeskUp, you will be able to instantly restore your entire workspace, from open applications to window layouts, so you can jump back into work without missing a beat.

Open Source

DeskUp is open source, giving you full transparency and control over how your workspaces are saved, loaded, and managed. With DeskUp, you will be able to customize and extend the app to fit your unique workflow perfectly.
